Baseball and its Rich History

America's Pastime

Baseball Rules

Baseball has a rich set of rules that have evolved over time.

  • The rules of baseball were formed in the 1800s
  • The first game was played in 1846
  • Baseball started in the United States

Historic Milestones

Some of the key milestones in baseball history:

  1. 1869: Cincinnati Red Stockings played first professional game
  2. 1876: National League formed, giving baseball structure
  3. 1903: American League formed, starting major rivalries

Baseball is celebrated for its impact on American culture and its ongoing evolution. It is enjoyed by players and fans of all ages across the country.

Fun fact: The game has influenced music, movies, and fashion throughout history!
