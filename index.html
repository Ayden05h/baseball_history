Baseball and its Rich History

Baseball

America's Pastime

Baseball is known as America's pastime. The game dates way back into the 18th century

Baseball has rich history and keeps evolving! Baseball is so diverse in culture it holds a great influence.

  • The rules of baseball were formed in the 1800's
    • The first game of baseball was played in 1846
  • Baseball was first played in the United States
    1. In 1869 the Cincinatti Red Stockings played the first game in baseball
    2. In 1876 The National league was formed giving baseball its structure
    3. In 1903 the American league was formed creating the first in sport rival of baseball
